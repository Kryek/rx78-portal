OVERVIEW

The Real Grade (RG) RX-78-2 Gundam (Ver. 2.0) is a 1/144 scale kit released in 2024.

TRIVIA

  • The first 1/144 scale Gunpla, and by extension, the first Real Grade kit, to bear the "Ver. 2.0" moniker.
  • The first RG to feature the cross-compatible rear mounting points first used on the HGAC XXXG-01W Wing Gundam.
  • Released to commemorate the 45th anniversary of the Mobile Suit Gundam franchise.
  • Like the 2020s Entry Grades and the High Grade the Witch from Mercury kits, most of the interior/exterior frame areas utilize a similar C-clip-styled construction, which is designed to be assembled once and then locked at a different angle.
  • Unlike the previous release, this RG RX-78-2 Gundam does not come with a standing 1/144 scale figurine.
