OVERVIEW

The Mega Size Model (MSM) RX-78-2 Gundam is a 1/48 scale kit released in 2010.

TRIVIA

  • Other than the panel-lining/weathering tutorial, the How-to-guide book also features a detailed build by Hiroyuki Noda (with Takuji Yamada doing the finishes) of Hobby Japan, and a Real Type Gundam modeled by Yamazaki Gunso of Dengeki Hobby.
  • A hidden Core Block panel can be seen at the E-1 runner. Bandai Hobby's staff also endorses any of the customizers to recreate the Core Fighter's docking feature or its hidden cockpit feature if needed.
  • A redecoed/repainted Mega Size Model Gundam, representing the enlarged/"Hyperized" unit from Mobile Suit Gundam GQuuuuuuX, was displayed at the All Japan Model & Hobby Show 2025. It was branded as a not-for-sale display piece.
