OVERVIEW

A collaboration item between BANDAI's Gunpla and ANA. The 1/48th scale 'Mega Size Model Gundam' in ANA's corporate colors with an image of a fresh sky.

TRIVIA

  • This kit was available on both domestic and international ANA × Gundam Sky Project flights between July 1st and February 28th, 2011. Only limited to 1 kit per passenger, while stock lasted.
