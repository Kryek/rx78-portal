OVERVIEW

The Perfect Grade (PG) RX-78-2 Gundam is a 1/60 scale kit released in 1998.

TRIVIA

  • Second Perfect Grade kit (after the Perfect Grade Evangelion in 1997) released in Bandai's history. Upon this release, the Perfect Grade line would be exclusive to the Gundam franchise until 2017, when Bandai released the Perfect Grade 1/72 scale Millennium Falcon for the Star Wars model kit series.
  • First Gunpla kit to use "Earth Federation Space Force (E.F.S.F.)" as the official moniker for the Earth Federation Forces.
  • Due to cost limitations, the Hyper Bazooka, the Beam Javelin, and the Gundam Hammer are omitted from this release. The weapons accessories would later be released in two different Custom Set boxes.
