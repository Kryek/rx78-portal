OVERVIEW

The SDEX RX-78-2 Gundam (001) is a 2015, low-cost kit offering improved, sleek proportions and better articulation than traditional SD kits.

TRIVIA

  • First release in the SDEX series and the first release from the Universal Century timeline.
  • A new gimmick added with these EX Standard kits is the ability to borrow parts and combine them to make new weapons that can be used with 1/144 scale HG Gundam kits.
