OVERVIEW

This version is a lottery-exclusive SD kit, released as part of Bandai Spirits’ Ichiban Kuji campaign celebrating Gunpla’s 40th anniversary (2020). It was only obtainable through “every ticket wins a prize” lottery draws at convenience stores, hobby shops, and select retailers.

TRIVIA

  • Japan-exclusive lottery item (not sold normally).
  • Designed to match the 40th anniversary Gunpla theme lineup.
