OVERVIEW

The Master Grade (MG) RX-78-02 Gundam (referred to as Gundam The Origin) is a 1/100 scale kit released in 2015.

TRIVIA

  • This was the first MG iteration of the RX-78-02 Gundam unit.
  • While it also comes with an additional late-type version of the Beam Rifle, the MG only features the early-type version of the unit.
