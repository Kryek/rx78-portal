OVERVIEW

The Master Grade (MG) RX-78-2 Gundam (Ver. Ka) is a 1/100 scale kit released in 2002.

TRIVIA

  • The third incarnation of the RX-78-2 Gundam in the MG line.
  • Based on Katoki Hajime's take on the Mobile Suit. (1994 B-Club Resin kit notwithstanding)
  • This was the first MG Gundam kit to have a fully transformable Core Fighter with a collapsible tail fin.
