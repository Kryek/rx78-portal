OVERVIEW

The Entry Grade (2020) RX-78F00/E Gundam is a 1/144 scale kit released in 2025.

TRIVIA

  • This Gunpla also doubles as the already discontinued RX-78F00 Gundam from Gundam Factory Yokohama.
  • It is the first Entry Grade iteration of both the RX-78F00 and the RX-78F00/E Gundam.
  • The Gunpla's proportions also bear some similarities to the SCM (Special Creative Model) EX RX-78-2 Gundam figure, a Banpresto product.
  • It is a new-mold model kit of the RX-78F00/RX-78F00/E Gundam unit that is not a Gundam Factory Yokohama exclusive after its closure.
