OVERVIEW

The Entry Grade (EG) (2020) RX-78-2 Gundam is a 1/144 scale kit released in 2020.

TRIVIA

  • This kit is revealed along with MG XXXG-00W0 Wing Gundam Zero EW (Ver.Ka) in Bandai's Hobby Next Phase Week.
  • Despite what the stock renders depict, the double-V mouth vents are sculpted grooves instead of silted.
  • The single-piece hand parts, while improving grip stability, prevent them from wielding accessories with non-open 3mm handles.
