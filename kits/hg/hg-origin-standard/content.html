OVERVIEW

The High Grade Gundam The Origin (HGGTO) RX-78-02 Gundam is a 1/144 scale kit released in 2020.

TRIVIA

  • This item was revealed along with the rest of the new Gunplas in The Gundam Base's "Next Phase: Road to 2020" event held on 28th December.
  • Barring the 1/144 B-Club kit released in 2005, this Gunpla is the very first HG iteration of the RX-78-02 Gundam unit from The Origin.
