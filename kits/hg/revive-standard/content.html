OVERVIEW

The High Grade Universal Century (HGUC) RX-78-2 Gundam (Revive) is a 1/144 scale kit released in 2015.

TRIVIA

  • Design wise, the Revive version of the HGUC RX-78-2 is a simplified version of the HGUC RX-78-2 Gundam (Ver G30th), with classic style feet design and the simpler backpack design similar with the HGUC 021 RX-78-2 Gundam.
  • Unlike the HG Ver.G30th kit, this Gunpla does not include the Gundam Hammer, but the accessory can be purchased in the "Gundam Hammer & Original Weapons" set, which was released as a freebie for the extra publication edition of the September 2015 issue of Gunpla ACE Magazine.
  • This Gunpla also does not include the Beam Javelin, but the accessory can be purchased in the "Beam Javelin & Original Weapons" set, which was released as a freebie for the extra publication edition of the September 2015 issue of Gundam ACE Magazine.
  • Unlike the first 2 HG Gundam kits, & like the HG G30th kit, this Gunpla does not include a non-transformable Core Fighter.
