OVERVIEW

The High Grade (HG) RX-78-2 Gundam (Ver.G30th) is a 1/144 scale kit released in 2009.

TRIVIA

  • This Gunpla is released to commemorate the 30th anniversary of Mobile Suit Gundam.
  • This was the first RX-78-2 Gundam kit designed after the 1/1 scale RX-78-2 Gundam statue, which has since become the standard design of most RX-78-2 Gundam kits.
