OVERVIEW

The High Grade Beyond Global (HGBG) RX-78-2 Gundam (Beyond Global) is a 1/144 scale kit released in 2020.

TRIVIA

  • First revealed at the poster featured in the Gundam.info page.
  • The first 1/144 RX-78-2 Gundam kit to resemble Kunio Okawara's design.
  • The Gunpla's proportions also bear some similarities to the SCM (Special Creative Model) EX RX-78-2 Gundam figure, a Banpresto product.
  • The Gunpla's coloration is similar to the O.Y.W. version of the MG RX-78-2 Gundam kit.
